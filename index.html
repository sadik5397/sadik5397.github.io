Questions we have answers
for your value question

What is QuickCV and how does it work? +

QuickCV is an AI-powered CV builder that helps you create professional resumes in minutes. Simply enter your information, choose a template, and our AI will help optimize your content for maximum impact with potential employers.

Is QuickCV free to use? +

Yes! We offer a free plan that includes basic CV creation with one template. For advanced features like AI-powered content, unlimited templates, and ATS optimization, we offer affordable Pro and Premium plans.

Are your templates ATS-friendly? +

Absolutely! All our templates are designed to be ATS (Applicant Tracking System) friendly, ensuring your CV passes through automated screening systems and reaches human recruiters.

Can I edit my CV after creating it? +

Yes! You can edit, update, and customize your CV anytime. Your CV is saved in your account and you can make unlimited changes with our Pro and Premium plans.

What formats can I download my CV in? +

You can download your CV in PDF format with our free plan. Pro and Premium users can also download in Word, HTML, and other formats for maximum flexibility.
